About Adrian

I grew up in Portland, OR and earned my Bachelors and Masters of Music in Operatic Performance at the University of Southern California. Since graduating, I have enjoyed a busy performance schedule across the country, but felt my innate curiousity and joy for working with technology was neglected. Moreover, the state of performing arts in the U.S., coupled with the national, and my personal, bloated student debt issues made me think seriously about expanding my skill-set to include something that would make me feel fulfilled and provide some security.

I thought that pursuing a course of study rooted in web development would offer me the well-roundedness I sought. Enrolling in Thinkful has proven to be one of the more fulfilling, joyful, and engaging choices I have made and I look forward to where my work in web development will take me!
